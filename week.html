Weekly To Do List

Manage a list of things "to do" in your week based on priority.

Enter the things you need do based priority and click the button to make your list

To Add Another Task to the end, use the "Add Task Function"

To Edit a Task to a different position, use the "Edit Tasks Function"

Once done a task, you may use the "Instert Postion Function" and insert a blank to replace the task that was at the position and make it blank
